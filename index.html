1. Create your profile

Record and upload your video profile in the app.

2. Take the video interview

Apply for job opportunities with just a click by using your virtual video-profile. Each job is promoted in a short video.

3. Receive a Video Reply from the company.

Take the interview remotely, in your own time. Interviews can be conducted asynchronously. You will be able to reply to the employer's interview questions before commencing to the final live-interview.

4. Celebrate! ...or try another one!

You can conduct the live interview with your employer remotely. The live interview is the final stage of your job application process. You can do your face-to-face interview on Cwebbing. Success!
